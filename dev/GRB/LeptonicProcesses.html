Leptonic Processes ​

Charged particle radiates electromagnetic waves when it is accelerated.

Due to the relativistic nature of GRBs, in the sites of GRB prompt emission and afterglow, the internal energy (effective temperature) is usually too high for the survival of any atoms. Usually we ignore the atom emission (absorption) lines.

Introduce three radiation mechanisms about charged particle accelerated in electromagnetic field, and pair production-annihilation that occur when high-energy photons are involved.
